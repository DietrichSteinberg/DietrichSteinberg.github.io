Led Wall Artist Statement

For the wall I made an LED effect that was inspired by James Turrell's artworks. The overall structure is inspired by Greeco-Roman archicteture. I chose to use a orangish color LEDs, because I wanted the structure to have a warm cozy vibe, and I also wanted to make sure I didn't pull too much voltage by using a more power hungry color LED. I used white paper on the back in order to diffuse the light to help create that warm cozy feeling.

Brushbot documentation

Artist Statement

The brush bot is meant to be an ironic parady of the ideal of the vitorian gentleman. Therefore I made it to look like a "gentleman" with a haggered appearance. For the gentleman part, I decorated it with a top hat and a cigerette, and for the haggered part, I purposely used the mismatching electronic parts to contruct a face and beard. The eyes were made with the green Led and the on/off switch. The brush of the brush bot looks like an unkempt beard.
