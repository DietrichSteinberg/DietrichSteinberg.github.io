Improbable Creature

❮ ❯

This a high poly count model of an imaginary createure that is the combination of a jumping spider, prey mantis and an ankylosaurus. It is a predator and a carnivore. It has a spider head, mandibles and eyes for optimal sight. The main body is that of a prey mantis. The tail is that of an Ankylosaurus. The “skin” is inspired by an octopus’ skin for camouflage. The environment that it lives in is a rocky arid desert with low gravity. The atmosphere is light and doesn’t effectively refract sunlight.
